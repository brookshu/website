About Me

I'm a third-year at Northwestern University, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Machine Learning & Data Science. The past few summers, I have interned with both private companies and academic institutions, gaining valuable experience across both sectors. In the past, my primary focus has been on backend development, specializing in computer vision, object detection, and image processing algorithms. I'm proficient in Python and JavaScript, and have experience with machine learning libraries such as PyTorch and TensorFlow.

I attended the University of Illinois Laboratory High School in Urbana, Illinois, where I was able to take advanced classes including Intro to Python and Databases with UIUC Professor Craig Zilles and Abdu Alawini respectively. In addition, I took General Chemistry virtually at UIUC during COVID, which gave me a taste of what college classes were like.

I'd like to say that I'm sociable and a strong team player, but is also adept at working independently. My 2025 summer internship was in an office largely composed of mechanical and systems engineers, which h ighlights my ability to collaborate with professionals outside his primary field and bridge the knowledge gap between the fields. A fun fact about me is that I'm an international citizen, as I primarily grew up in the U.S., spent a year in France, and attended summer school for years in Japan.

I've also played Minecraft since elementary school and continue to play in college with my friends when I have time. I recently also just got EAFC 2024, and I'm hoping to play it soon.
